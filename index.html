⚙️ What Is a Gaming PC?

A gaming PC is built to deliver high performance for video games. It’s customizable, powerful, and future-proof.

🧱 Core Components

PartDescription
GPUHandles visuals & frame rates
CPURuns game logic and background tasks
RAMAllows smooth multitasking
StorageHolds your games and OS
MotherboardConnects all components
PSUPowers the system
CoolingPrevents overheating
CaseHolds everything and supports airflow
